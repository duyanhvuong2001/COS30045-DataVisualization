Sustainability: Energy


As technology and humanity approaches the 21st Century, more and more emphasis is put on the usage of energy in everyday lives.
As such, there are many ways to generate energy, whether if its in an environmentally friendly way or not. The question that we
should have in an energy-dependent world is, "How much of our energy is generated without harming the environment?"



This website seeks to educate and provide information on how much of our energy used is clean energy,
along with some statistics on how much the distribution of energy generation has changed per year in Australia.
